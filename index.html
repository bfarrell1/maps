Map of College Volleyball Arenas

by Brendan Farrell

This map shows the locations of top volleyball teams that do not share their arena with the university's men's basketball team. For example, despite being ranked 11th, the Gators are not on this map because they share their home arena with the men's basketball team. This is meant to show which teams play in an arena designed with volleyball in mind.

You can find Brendan's other work at his Github page.
